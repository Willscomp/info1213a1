Deep Pro History:

Deep Production started in September 2015. At that time, the information in 2012 message was making some of the elderly of the church confused. So a few co-workers and I got together and try to explain it to these these members. However, as we explain, we found out that majority of the church did not understand. So we thought of a way to explain it shortly through some of the difficult points. And for us to explain it to everyone without having to say it over and over again, we put it in a video.
